About Us

What Do You Know About Us?

With humble beginnings in 2004, Fairfax & Carter has been a trail blazer in business restructuring, debt restructuring, capital advisory, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), raising capital, deal management within Africa and the Middle East. With over two decades of expertise, we have cultivated a reputation for delivering strategic solutions that drive sustainable growth and value creation for our clients across diverse industries.

Global Focus

While operating globally, F&C has developed deep-rooted expertise and extensive networks within Africa, the Middle East, and Emerging Markets. Our localized insights, coupled with a global perspective, enable us to navigate diverse market dynamics, cultural nuances, and regulatory environments, delivering tailored solutions that resonate with regional and international clients alike.

Our Client Profile

F&C caters to a diverse clientele comprising multinational corporations, financial institutions, private equity firms, family-owned businesses, government entities, and entrepreneurs. Our collaborative approach, industry-specific knowledge, and commitment to excellence have earned us the trust and loyalty of clients seeking strategic guidance and value-driven solutions
